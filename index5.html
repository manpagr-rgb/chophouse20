Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Delivery typically takes 30-45 minutes within Idah town. During peak hours, delivery may take up to 60 minutes. You can track your order live on the Tracking page.
What's the minimum order amount?
The minimum order amount for delivery is ₦1,500. Orders below this amount will not be processed for delivery. You can pickup in-store without minimum order.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Bank Transfer to our Moniepoint account (2039485732) and Card Payments via Paystack secure gateway.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placement. Please contact our support team immediately via WhatsApp or phone.
Do you offer vegetarian options?
Yes! We offer several vegetarian options including our Special Jollof Rice, Fried Rice, and Moin Moin. You can customize your order in the modal.
What if my payment verification fails?
If your payment verification fails, you can submit your payment details and screenshot through the Tracking page. Our support team will review and confirm your order. You can also contact us directly via WhatsApp or phone.
